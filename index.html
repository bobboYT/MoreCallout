Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about More Callout.

🔍
The plugin doesn't load, what should I do? ▼
Make sure you have all requirements installed: GTA V, LSPDFR, Rage Plugin Hook, and Script Hook V. Place the .dll and .ini files in Plugins/LSPDFR/ folder. Restart Rage Plugin Hook after installation.
How often is More Callout updated? ▼
More Callout is updated regularly every Saturday at 4:00 PM (CEST). Updates may include new callouts, improvements, optimizations, and bug fixes. If critical issues are reported, additional hotfix updates may be released during the week.
Does More Callout impact FPS? ▼
No. More Callout is designed to be lightweight and optimized, so it should not have a significant impact on performance during normal gameplay. Performance may still vary depending on the number of installed plugins and your PC's hardware.
Which versions of GTA V are compatible with More Callout? ▼
More Callout is compatible with the latest version of GTA V, provided you are also using the latest versions of LSPDFR and RagePluginHook. We recommend keeping all components up to date to ensure maximum compatibility.
Can I use More Callout with other callout plugins? ▼
Yes! More Callout is fully compatible with other callout plugins like StopThePed, Ultimate Backup, and others. Each plugin manages its own callouts independently.
How do I disable a specific callout? ▼
Open the MoreCallout.ini file in Plugins/LSPDFR/ and customize each callout individually. Set a specific callout to true or false to enable or disable it, and adjust its spawn rate if needed.
What's the difference between Stable and Beta versions? ▼
Stable versions are thoroughly tested and recommended for normal gameplay. Beta versions contain new features and callouts but may have bugs or compatibility issues. Use Beta only for testing and feedback.
How do I change the language? ▼
Open MoreCallout.ini and change the Language setting to 'Italian' or 'English'. The change takes effect after restarting LSPDFR. All callout texts and UI elements will switch to your chosen language.
Does EasyMode affect all callouts? ▼
EasyMode makes suspects less aggressive and limits their weapons to basic options only. It applies to most combat-related callouts but doesn't affect accident or medical emergency scenarios. You can enable it in the .ini file.
Where can I report bugs or suggest new callouts? ▼
Join our Discord server! The link is in the footer of this page. You can report bugs, suggest new callout ideas, get support from the community, and stay updated on upcoming releases.
