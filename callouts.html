📋 Public Order & Common Reports 13

📌
Abandoned Vehicle
A car left parked in the same spot for days, potentially stolen, missing license plates, or creating a hazard.
📌
Civilian Fight
Two or more citizens involved in a physical altercation or a heated verbal shouting match in a public area.
📌
Intoxicated Person
An individual who is visibly drunk or high, acting disorderly, and disturbing passersby.
📌
Noise Complaint
A report regarding extremely loud music, shouting, or late-night disturbances coming from a residence or local venue.
📌
Suspicious Person
An individual loitering furtively around residential homes or businesses, looking through windows or scouting properties.
📌
Trespassing
Someone who has entered or refuses to leave private property, a construction site, or a restricted area.
📌
Welfare Check
A request to check on a citizen who hasn't been heard from in days or who is acting highly unusual, ensuring their safety.
📌
Public Disturbance
Shouting, fighting, or rowdy behavior that disrupts the peace and quiet of a neighborhood.
📌
Out-of-control party
A private party that has escalated with too many guests, excessive alcohol consumption, loud music, and potential fights.
📌
Bar fight
A violent brawl inside or just outside a bar/pub, often fueled by alcohol, with risks of flying bottles or broken furniture.
📌
Violent fans
Aggressive groups of sports fans causing chaos, riots, or vandalism near a stadium or in city streets.
📌
Protest turned violent
A peaceful demonstration that degenerates into clashes with law enforcement, property damage, and rioting.
📌
Harassing homeless person
A transient individual aggressively panhandling, yelling at pedestrians, or blocking the entrance to a business.

🚗 Traffic & Road Safety 10

🚗
Minor Traffic Accident
A fender bender between vehicles with property damage only and no severe injuries; drivers might be arguing.
🚗
Vehicle Obstruction
A car double-parked, blocking a driveway, or stopped dead in the middle of a lane, heavily disrupting traffic.
🚗
DUI / Drunk driving
A motorist driving erratically, swerving between lanes, or committing severe traffic violations under the suspected influence of alcohol or drugs.
🚗
Stolen vehicle pursuit
A flagged stolen vehicle is spotted by police, and the driver refuses to pull over, initiating a high-speed pursuit.
🚗
Illegal street racing
Modified cars racing at high speeds on public roads, endangering pedestrians and oncoming traffic.
🚗
Taxi with aggressive passenger
A cab driver requesting immediate assistance because a passenger is being violent, threatening, or refusing to pay.
🚗
Suspicious vehicle stopped roadside
A vehicle parked in a dark, isolated area with its headlights off, with occupants seemingly hiding or doing something illicit.
🚗
Person wandering on the highway
A pedestrian walking dangerously along the shoulder or attempting to cross a high-speed freeway.
🚗
Animal on the road
A large animal (like a deer or a stray dog) wandering onto the roadway, creating a severe crash hazard.
⭐
HighSpeedPursuit NEW
Dynamic pursuit with multiple routes, roadblocks, and suspect behavior variations.

💰 Property Crimes (Theft & Burglary) 6

💰
Simple Robbery
A theft committed using force, intimidation, or direct threats against a victim (like a purse snatching), but without firearms.
💰
Car theft in progress
A suspect caught in the act of breaking into a vehicle, smashing windows, or hotwiring it to drive away.
💰
Residential Burglary
A break-in occurring (or just occurred) at a private house, apartment, or residential estate.
💰
Commercial Burglary
A forced entry or "smash-and-grab" at a store, office, or shopping mall, typically during closing hours.
💰
Garage Burglary
A break-in targeting a garage, carport, or storage shed to steal tools, bicycles, or motorcycles.
⭐
BankRobbery NEW
Armed robbery of a financial institution with hostage potential and multi-phase getaway.

🔴 Severe & Violent Crimes 9

🔴
Armed Person
Reports of an individual openly carrying or brandishing a firearm, knife, or other dangerous weapon in a public space, causing panic.
🔴
Wanted Person Spotted
An officer or a citizen recognizes a suspect on the street who has an active arrest warrant.
🔴
Stabbing
A violent assault where a victim has been injured or actively threatened with a knife or sharp object.
🔴
Assault
A physical attack on an innocent person, with injuries being inflicted in real-time or just before police arrival.
🔴
Domestic violence
A violent family dispute inside a residence involving active physical altercations or severe threats between partners or relatives.
🔴
Armed wanted suspect
Locating a dangerous, wanted fugitive who is confirmed to be armed and likely to shoot to avoid capture.
⭐
ActiveShooter NEW
Multiple armed suspects in a public location with panicked civilians and ongoing casualties.
⭐
Officer Down NEW
An injured NPC officer is found on the ground. EMS is automatically dispatched while the suspect attempts to flee.
⭐
HostageSituation NEW
Hostage negotiation through dialogue with escalating demands and tactical options.

💊 Narcotics & Drugs 2

💊
Drug dealing
Active, ongoing sale of illegal substances in a specific area, such as a local park, alleyway, or street corner.
💊
Suspicious drug exchange
Two individuals caught performing a quick hand-to-hand exchange of cash for small packages before quickly walking away.

🏥 Medical & Psychiatric Emergencies 3

🏥
Missing person
A search operation for an individual (often a minor or an elderly person with medical conditions) who has disappeared and is at risk.
🏥
Person experiencing a psychotic episode
An individual undergoing a severe mental health crisis, exhibiting delusions, hallucinations, and unpredictable behavior.
🏥
Attempted suicide
A person threatening to take their own life (e.g., standing on a bridge or rooftop ledge), requiring immediate crisis negotiation.

🚨 Major Accidents & Disasters (Joint Fire/EMS Response) 7

🚨
Serious traffic accident
A high-speed crash resulting in heavily damaged vehicles, victims trapped inside wreckage, and critical medical injuries.
🚨
Motorcycle accident
A severe crash involving a motorcyclist who has been thrown from their bike, usually resulting in major trauma.
🚨
Overturned truck
A semi-truck or heavy commercial vehicle that has flipped over, blocking multiple lanes and potentially spilling cargo.
🚨
Train accident
A train derailment or a violent collision between a locomotive and a vehicle stuck at a railroad crossing.
🚨
Small plane crash
A light aircraft or private plane that has gone down in an urban area, field, or forest.
🚨
Car in the river
A vehicle that has veered off the road and plunged into a body of water, leaving occupants at risk of drowning.
🚨
Fuel leak
A massive spill of gasoline, diesel, or hazardous materials from a tanker or ruptured fuel tank, posing an extreme fire hazard.

💣 Explosives & Arson Threats 6

💣
Bomb threat
An anonymous tip or call claiming an explosive device has been planted inside a public building, school, or landmark.
💣
Arson
A suspect caught intentionally setting fire to a trash dumpster, vehicle, or structure.
💣
False fire alarm
The malicious or accidental pulling of a fire alarm lever in a public building when no actual emergency exists.
💣
Suspicious package
An unattended box, backpack, or sealed container left in a crowded area (like a transit station) that could be an IED.
💣
Abandoned briefcase
A stray briefcase left behind on a bench or post office counter, treated with extreme caution due to explosive risks.
💣
Reported explosion
A loud blast followed by smoke reported by multiple citizens, requiring immediate dispatch to investigate casualties and damage.

🐕 Animals & Backup 2

🐕
Callout Helpers
A call for immediate backup from fellow officers who are outnumbered, facing resistance, or handling a volatile scene.
🐕
Aggressive dog
A large, loose dog without a leash or muzzle that is growling at, cornering, or actively attacking pedestrians.
